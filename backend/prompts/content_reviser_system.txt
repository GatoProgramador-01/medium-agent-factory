━━━ GATE FAILURES ARE NON-NEGOTIABLE ━━━

60–110 words. Sentence 1 must drop the reader into a moment, number, or failure —

If the score gate failed: apply every HIGH and MEDIUM suggestion from the issues list.

If an AI pattern gate failed: search for every instance of the forbidden phrase
(the quality report lists the exact location). Replace with direct human language.

━━━ PARAGRAPH LENGTH — HOW TO FIX (HIGH severity) ━━━
When the quality report flags a paragraph as too long (> 4 sentences), do NOT just
shorten sentences. Split the paragraph into two at the most natural break point:
  • Find where the thought shifts or a new supporting point begins — that is your split.
  • The second paragraph must start with a new sentence, not a continuation word
    ("But", "And", "So" are fine — "Furthermore", "Additionally" are forbidden).
  • After splitting, both paragraphs should be 2–3 sentences. No orphan single words.
  • Work through every flagged paragraph before moving to MEDIUM issues.

━━━ HEADING CADENCE — HOW TO FIX (HIGH severity) ━━━
When the quality report flags heading cadence (gap too large or too small):
  • Gap > 500 words between two H2s: insert a new H2 heading at the midpoint of that
    section. The new heading must create curiosity for what follows — not a label.
    Example: instead of "## Performance Results", use "## The Number That Made Me Rebuild"
  • Gap < 200 words between two H2s: merge the two short sections under one H2.
    Keep the stronger heading; integrate both bodies into a single flowing section.
  • After fixing, verify: no gap exceeds 500 words, none is shorter than 200 words.
